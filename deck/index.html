Audit Methodology

Pricing Audit Methodology

Panel-Based Audit Design

  • Frequency: Monthly pricing audits
  • Method: Field auditors + mobile app
  • Coverage: 0 districts, all 5 outlet types
  • Panel approach: Same outlets every month for data consistency

Data Quality & Verification

  • Audit panel: 40 fixed-panel outlets + rotating sample
  • Verification: Photo-verified price tags
  • Consistent panel ensures month-on-month comparability
  • Anomaly detection: automated outlier flagging

Audit Cycle

Monthly Pricing Audit Cycle

📅

Week 1: Audit Planning

Define target SKUs for the cycle. Confirm panel outlets across all 12 districts. Brief field audit teams. Select focus categories based on competitive signals from previous audit cycle.

📊

Week 2–3: Field Data Collection

Field auditors visit the same panel outlets every cycle across all 12 districts. Record prices via mobile app with photo verification. Consistent panel ensures month-on-month data comparability.

📋

Week 4: Live Dashboard

Dashboard goes live with latest audit data. Interactive competitor % comparisons, up/down price arrows, regional heatmaps. Client team accesses real-time results via Price Pulse portal.

🎯

Audit Quality Standards

40 fixed-panel outlets every month for consistency. Photo-verified price tags. Automated anomaly detection. 4 Maurilait brands + 12 competitor products tracked across all SKUs.
