Inflation Narrative

Mauritius experienced a sharp food inflation spike to 9.2% in mid-2025, driven by rupee depreciation (30% since 2020) and global commodity price increases. While overall inflation has moderated to 3.5% by February 2026, the structural vulnerability remains: Mauritius imports 75% of all food consumed, making the island uniquely exposed to global price shocks and currency movements. For a dairy processor importing 96-98% of raw materials, price monitoring is existential.

Weekly Audit Cadence

Week 1
Audit Planning & SKU Selection
Define target SKUs for the cycle based on competitive signals from previous audit. Confirm panel outlets across all 12 districts. Brief field audit teams on focus areas and any special collection requirements. Select priority categories for deeper competitive analysis.
Week 2 – 3
Field Data Collection
Trained field auditors visit the same panel outlets every cycle. Prices recorded via mobile application with photo-verified price tags. Real-time GPS validation confirms auditor presence at panel outlets. Automated outlier flagging catches data entry errors in-field. Coverage spans all 5 outlet types: supermarkets, hypermarkets, corner shops, convenience stores, and wholesalers.
Week 4
Dashboard Goes Live
Price Pulse dashboard updated with latest audit data. Interactive competitor % comparisons with visual up/down arrows. Regional heatmaps, brand trend lines, and anomaly alerts all refresh simultaneously. Client team accesses results in real-time via the Price Pulse portal — no waiting for static reports.
